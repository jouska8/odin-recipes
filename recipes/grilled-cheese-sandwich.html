Grill Cheese Sandwich

Description

A grilled cheese sandwich is a classic comfort food, made by toasting bread with cheese until melted and golden brown.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, etc.)
  • 1 tbsp butter or margarine

Steps

  1. Butter bread: Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Assemble: Place cheese between the non-buttered sides of the bread.
  3. Grill: Heat a skillet over medium heat. Place the sandwich in the pan, buttered side down.
  4. Cook: Cook until golden brown, flip, and cook the other side until cheese is melted.
  5. Serve: Slice and enjoy warm
